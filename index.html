Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how ArmAPPs Armenia handles information. Default language: English.

1. Introduction

ArmAPPs Armenia is a mobile application that provides a directory/catalog of Armenian apps categorized by type. The app is a browsing and discovery tool — users do not need to register or log in to use the application.

2. No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or process personal user data within the app. There is no user login, and we do not create or maintain user profiles. If you use the app purely to browse the catalog, no personal information is recorded by ArmAPPs Armenia.

3. What the App May Show

The app displays public information about third-party applications (such as app names, descriptions, screenshots, developer names, and links). That information is either provided by the app developers or publicly available from app stores or developer-provided assets.

4. External Links

The catalog may include links that open external websites, app store pages, or third‑party services. ArmAPPs Armenia is not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those external sites. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third party you visit.

5. Analytics and Advertising

The current version of the app does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs. If this changes in a future update, a clear notice will be added to the app and this Privacy Policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Data Retention

Because no personal user data is collected, we do not retain personal data. Any public app metadata displayed in the catalog is fetched from developer sources or app stores and may be cached temporarily to improve performance.

7. Children

The app is not directed at children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect personal information, there is no intentional collection from children. If you believe a child’s personal information has been provided to us, please contact us and we will take appropriate action.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The effective date will be updated when changes are made. Significant changes will be communicated in the app release notes when possible.
